Our Story

CurtyHair is a one-of-a-kind hair salon that celebrates the beauty and diversity of kinky curly hair. We train our stylists to master the art of the many hair textures that are as wonderfully unique as our community here in Houston. We provide a personalized experience, delivering custom hair color, cuts, and styling with at-home maintenance lessons to be sure everyone is able to recreate their gorgeous new hairstyle every day of the week.

Our Team
